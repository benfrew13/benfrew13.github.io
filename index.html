    longshot2025

    Coined from a tendency towards sniping in FPS games, the idiomatic term, and this Popular Science cover article that eight-year-old me thought looked totally badass. I tacked 2025 on to my first email address because my name by itself was already taken, and since then it's outlasted all my other usernames out of a sense of consistency. I'm sure I'll regret this decision in eleven years, but it's unfortunately grown on me.

    I use this on gaming services, and pretty much anywhere else where I need a handle other than my real name. As far as I know, nothing else online uses this exact string for anything. So if you see longshot2025 somewhere, it's probably me.
